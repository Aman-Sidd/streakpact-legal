Account Deletion

Delete Your Account

You can request permanent deletion of your StreakPact account and all associated data at any time.

⚠️ Before You Continue

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Your Reliability Score, Week Streak, and all history will be deleted
  • All your check-in photos and proof will be permanently removed
  • You will be removed from all active and pending pacts
  • Your pact members will be notified that you have left
  • Your display name and profile will no longer be visible to anyone
Step 1

Send a Deletion Request

Send an email to our support address from the email associated with your StreakPact account. This is how we verify your identity before deleting your data.

✉️
streakpact.official@gmail.com Send your request to this address
Use this subject line Delete My StreakPact Account – [Your Display Name]
Step 2

What to Include in Your Email

To process your request quickly, include the following in your email:

  • → The email address registered to your account
  • → Your display name in the app
  • → Confirmation that you want your account and all data permanently deleted
Step 3

What Happens Next

1

We receive your request

You'll get a confirmation reply within 48 hours acknowledging your deletion request.

2

Identity verification

We verify the request came from the account owner by matching the email address.

3

Data deletion

Your account, profile, check-in history, photos, pact memberships, and all associated data are permanently deleted within 7 days.

4

Confirmation sent

We'll send a final confirmation email once deletion is complete.

Data Retained

What Data Is Kept After Deletion

Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed. No personally identifiable information is retained after the deletion process is complete.

Aggregate, anonymised usage statistics (not linked to your identity) may be retained for service improvement purposes.
